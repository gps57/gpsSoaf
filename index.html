Why Accept a Coding Challenge?

Coding challenges can be used to highlight specific skills that might not come up in a job interview. Moreover, interview questions aren't always structured in a way that reveals the hidden strengths of your coding abilities. A fully implemented solution to a coding challenge, on the other hand, can target specific competencies and demonstrate them as they are applied to specific coding ojectives.
