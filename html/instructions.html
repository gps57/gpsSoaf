How To Use My Solution

The instructions below show you how to use my solution to the Sum of All Fears coding challenge. I hope you enjoy it.
  • When you are ready to start, click on the Solution option in the nav menu. Or click this link: My Solution
  • You will be presented with two data entry fields. Both will contain sample data. You can just click the "Find Solution" button now to see the results.
  • If you would like to see the results for a different list of numbers, simple enter the numbers you want to check into the field. Each number should be separated by a comma.
  • If you would like to change the K number, simply enter a whole number into the "K Number" field that is within the range 1 to 100.
  • After you are happy with the numbers you have entered, click the "Find Solution" button to see the results. The results will be presented below the large separating line.
  • There may be more that one solution for a single K number, depending on how many numbers you include in your list of numbers.
